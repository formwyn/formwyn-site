The Overcharge

This one doesn't build up to a big hit. It just never stops hitting. Meet **The Overcharge**.

Charged Bolts, Flame Shield (Flash), Unstable Currents (Boundless), Ceaseless Conduit. Sustained burst, forgiving.

Confirmed

Full build detail

Provisional

Skill, gear, and Paragon detail below is sourced from Icy Veins' Crackling Energy Sorcerer guide, last updated May 17, 2026 - before Season 14 launched (June 30, 2026). The underlying skills/aspects/Talisman system haven't had a season-specific rework announced, so the build's core identity is likely unchanged, but this hasn't been directly re-confirmed for Season 14 yet. Treat as a strong starting point, not gospel - we'll upgrade this to Confirmed once a Season 14-dated source verifies it. Exact numeric skill-tree point allocations aren't included here: Icy Veins renders their skill planner as an interactive graphic rather than text, so we can't reliably transcribe precise point counts per skill without risking errors - what's listed below is which skills matter and why, which we can verify with confidence.

Skill bar

  • Charged Bolts — Main spam skill - stand close to elites/bosses to hit with all bolts at once. Triggers Aspect of Elemental Attunement and the Boundless mechanic on Unstable Currents.
  • Flame Shield — Defensive skill modified by Flash - generates Crackling Energy rapidly while granting Movement Speed and Dodge Chance.
  • Ice Armor — Grants a large Barrier, bonus Armor, and Unstoppable. Refreshes on hitting enough enemies - keep it up as much as possible.
  • Teleport — Mobility and enemy-grouping tool via Raiment of the Infinite - pulls enemies toward you on cast.
  • Frost Nova — Generates Mana, applies Vulnerable, and Freezes enemies - Frozen targets shatter via Azurewrath.
  • Unstable Currents — Ultimate - automatically triggers Chain Lightning, Lightning Spear, and Ball Lightning (do not equip these three on the bar directly).

Key passives

  • Ceaseless Conduit - sustaining Crackling Energy charges even at high Attack Speed maintains a 4x damage multiplier; this is the build's core damage engine.
  • Enchantment Master - slot Ball Lightning in the first Enchantment Slot (triggered by Charged Bolts hits) and Teleport in the second for extra mobility.

Runewords

  • Cir + Vex - builds Offering by casting skills, then spends it for +1 to all Skills for 10s.
  • Yul + Mot - builds Offering on cooldown-skill casts, then spends it to gain a damage-reducing shadow.

Talisman (Seal & Charms)

  • Prioritize a Legendary Seal with a +1 Charm Slot affix (6 total Charm slots).
  • Insert all 5 pieces of Cain's Wild Lightning (more area damage) or Tal Rasha's Threefold Way (more defense) - prioritize Charms with extra Ranks to Shock Skills.
  • Insert Azurewrath to explode enemies Frozen by Frost Nova.
