Cable management for small desks

In a small room, trailing cables are the first thing you see walking in. This is the cheapest, highest-impact category on the whole list.

Best starting point: a single under-desk cable tray — ~£15-£25

Budget mesh trays start around £15; premium retractable versions run closer to £25. This catches the power strip and bulk cabling in one go.

Best value: a complete kit rather than piecing it together — ~£28-£35

Buying a tray, ties, clips, and sleeves separately adds up to roughly £46 in total; bundled kits covering the same ground typically run £28-35.

Worth adding once the basics are sorted

Adhesive cable clips running down the desk leg to the floor socket — a few pounds, and the difference between "tidy" and "actually tidy."
