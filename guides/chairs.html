Best gaming chairs for small UK rooms

Most gaming chair guides assume a sprawling setup. In a UK box room, footprint and a folding footrest matter as much as ergonomics.

Best overall: Racingreat Ergonomic Gaming Chair — sub-£55

Compact seat and base footprint, no bulky footrest, adjustable seat height, reclining backrest, headrest pillow and lumbar cushion at the price.

Best value with a footrest: Symino Gaming Chair

Feature-rich with footrest and full lumbar support — the footrest folds away when not in use, which matters in a tight room.

Best for the smallest bedrooms: bigzzia Gaming Chair — under £50

No footrest, which keeps both the price and the footprint down. The honest pick when the room simply won't take anything bigger.

Best solid build under £90: SONGMICS Racing Gaming Chair OBG073BH08

The most solid construction found under £90 in current UK comparisons.
