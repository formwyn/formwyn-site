Multi-use furniture for small and shared UK rooms

In a small room, floor space is the actual constraint, not budget. Furniture that does two jobs is worth more than furniture that does one job cheaply.

Best pound-for-pound buy: Dunelm storage ottoman — ~£32-£35

Doubles as seating, a coffee table, and hidden storage for bedding or shoes all in one piece — the single best multi-use purchase in this category.

Wall-mounted drop-leaf table: IKEA NORBERG range — price varies significantly by size and retailer

Folds flat against the wall when not in use, useful for a tiny kitchen or a fold-away desk. Pricing on this one genuinely varies a lot between IKEA direct and third-party resellers — check IKEA's own listing before buying anywhere else.

What we look for

Anything that serves two purposes — sleep and storage, seating and storage, dining and display — beats a single-purpose piece of the same price.
