Best mini PCs for tight rooms

This category moves fast — mini PC pricing and specs shift with component cycles, so treat this as a starting shortlist to check current pricing against, not a fixed table.

Best overall for a small room: Geekom AX8 Pro

Genuinely small footprint, real gaming capability (not just a media-box PC pretending to game), sensible price for what it does.

Step-up option: GEEKOM A9 Max

Higher-end components in a still-compact chassis; handles popular titles like Fortnite, Valorant, and FIFA-style games comfortably at 1080p. Worth it if the room genuinely can't fit a tower.

Honest caution — budget option: Blackview MP200

Good value for general use, but flagged plainly: no dedicated graphics card and weak cooling means it will disappoint anyone expecting real gaming performance.

Premium: Corsair One i500

For someone with real budget and a genuine small-space constraint, not a compromise pick.
