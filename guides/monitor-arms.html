Best monitor arms and cable management for small desks

On a desk under 120cm wide, a monitor arm frees more usable surface than almost any other single purchase.

Best overall: Ergotron LX — ~£130

Smooth gas-spring movement, 25kg load capacity, fits monitors up to 34in. Cables route through channels in the arm secured by detachable cladding. The arm 90% of UK buyers should get.

Design-conscious alternative: Herman Miller Flo Plus

Ultra-clean appearance with cable channels integrated into the design, 12-year warranty. A premium pick, not a starting recommendation.

If a full arm isn't in budget yet

A cable tray, cable spine, and a monitor riser is often steadier than a heavy arm balanced on a lightweight desk anyway — worth doing in that order rather than stretching for the arm first.
