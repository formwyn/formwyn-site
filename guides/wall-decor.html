No-drill wall décor and shelving for rented rooms

This is the category renters worry about most — bare walls, no permission to drill, and a deposit on the line. Adhesive strips and shelves genuinely hold real weight, within limits.

Best for hanging art: Command Picture Hanging Extra Large Strips (Set of 10) — ~£13

Large strips hold up to 7.2kg; Command's small and medium strips hold 1.8kg and 5.4kg respectively — match the strip size to the frame, not just the biggest pack available. Removes cleanly with no holes.

Best for shelving: no-drill adhesive floating shelf — from ~£21.79 (RRP ~£33)

Adhesive-mounted, no bracket or drilling. Only suitable for genuinely lightweight items — books and solid ornaments belong on a drilled shelf, not this one.

The honest caveat

Adhesive strips can occasionally pull paint off on removal, particularly on older or poorly-painted walls. Test on a hidden patch first if the wall's paint job looks fragile.
